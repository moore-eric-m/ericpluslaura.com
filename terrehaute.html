Our Story

Terre Haute, IN

That summer, they packed up the Escort one more time and moved back to the Midwest. Laura became an English professor at a community college in Terre Haute, IN, and Eric began taking classes in computer science. In October of that year, they got married at the Vigo County Courthouse, just the two of them and a judge with rad orange sneaks. Since then they’ve taken more trips to new cities like Indianapolis and Louisville, bought their first (and second) Christmas tree, drank a lot of tea, and watched a lot of sci-fi on Netflix. They’ve been planning their wedding celebration for a little while now, and can’t wait for everyone to celebrate together!
