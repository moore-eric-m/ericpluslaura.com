Our Story

Kalamazoo, MI

Here’s Laura: And here's Eric: Laura and Eric met as roommates at Fletcher, a coop house in Kalamazoo, where they both went to college. They started dating after the house’s Halloween party, trying (not very hard) to keep it a secret while spending a lot of time together, planning trips to Chicago and building forts. That spring, Laura got accepted to go to graduate school in Washington, and after a lot of very long talks, they decided to move together. In August 2011 they drove 2,000 miles in Eric’s Ford Escort and ended up in Spokane, WA.
