Our Story

Spokane, WA

The next two years were busy and crazy and awesome. Eric worked full time at Spokane Community College and Laura earned her MFA in fiction while working at Spokane Public Radio. They hiked some beautiful mountains, ate a lot of really good food, and traveled to awesome PNW places like Seattle, Missoula, and Portland. It was a whirlwind two years, and out there in the Northwest, they felt like they truly cemented their relationship. After Laura graduated, the two took a week-long trip to Vancouver, BC. One afternoon hiking through the gorgeous redwood forest of Stanley Park, Eric got down on one knee, said some very romantic things, and the two got engaged!
