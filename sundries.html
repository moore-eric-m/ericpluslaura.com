A Note on Gifts

We’ve lived together for almost five years and moved across the country twice, so by now our household is fairly complete! Plus, we are always thinking about our next move, so we like to keep things light (and able to fit in the Escort). If you are the gift-giving type and you have the perfect thing in mind for us, please feel free. If you feel comfortable giving us a cash gift, we’d love it! If you’re all about bringing the love and the set of hands for our celebration weekend, we’ll take that too!
